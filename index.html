Welcome to ISTE SNIST

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.

The ISTE Student's Chapter at Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology is the biggest of its kind in the country. ISTE-SNIST have always been striving for excellence in every venture, emphasizing teamwork at all levels. The strength of ISTE is the strong base it has in technical education institutions in the country. By demonstrating integrity, We look forward to explore the technological world.
